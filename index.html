Google Nyāra Document
  • Nyāra is a constructed language inspired by some traits of Japanese. It is a conlang that will be spoken or written by furries or weebs in many projects of internet or art such as songs, poems, fictional worlds, tweets, reddit posts, stories. If you are not both in theses communities but you are interested you can even join.
  • The more the conlang will be used the more it will evolve; This (discord) server serves to know how much people is interested and to work on the language together. The project is more or less serious, you can take it seriously or can take it as a fun thing, but really would love to see that the project will become real.
